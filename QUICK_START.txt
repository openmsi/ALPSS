SIGNAL DETECTION TIPS:

  Otsu (default):
    - Works well for most signals
    - Set "carrier_band_time" to ~10% of data duration

  IQ Analysis:
    - Better for weak or noisy signals
    - Adjust "iq_threshold_factor" (0.3-0.6 typical)

  CUSUM:
    - Best for sharp transitions
    - Tune "cusum_offset" and "cusum_threshold"
